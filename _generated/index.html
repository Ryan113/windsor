Memory Care for Seniors

Located in East Vancouver, Washington Windsor Gardens is an assisted living facility that sits on the former site of a lush botanical garden that once graced the pages of ‘National Geographic’. We specialize in caring for individuals living with memory loss such as Dementia and Alzheimer’s. 


At Windsor Gardens, the warm, elegant environment will invite you in. Windsor Gardens offers a secure, gated environment, ensuring the safety of your loved one. From the dedicated staff to the wonderful atmosphere, it is a truly great place to call home. You will find that the tranquil setting at Windsor Gardens brings comfort. Our one-acre park-like community invites you to enjoy beautiful walking paths and peaceful sitting areas amidst old trees and varied shrubbery. These commodities allow our residents to enjoy the outdoors in spring and summer in an unrestricted, yet safe and secure environment.

Windsor Gardens offers all of its residents services that include:

- 24/7 memory care and assistance from a care staff trained specifically for dementia and Alzheimer’s care
- On-call registered nurse
- Medication administration from licensed medication aides
- Delicious seasonal menus designed by a professional chef to meet all dietary needs and help stimulate appetite
- Daily in-house activity program offering opportunities for companionship, mental stimulation and exercise
- Assistance with personal hygiene, including bathing, grooming, dressing, and incontinence management
- Closed-circuit cameras and a fenced/gated perimeter offer safety and security to residents
- Secured and inviting outdoor garden and verandas where residents and their families can gather
- Fully monitored fire protection and sprinkler system
- Professional housekeeping staff providing daily linens and personal laundry services 
- In addition to long-term residential care, Windsor Gardens offers respite care (when available) and adult day care services.
