Our Community

What differentiates Windsor Gardens from other memory care communities?

Unique Size:

 Windsor Gardens is a 12-bed assisted living facility that specializes in providing memory care for seniors. We are the only memory care facility of this size in all of Clark County. Other care environments are typically much smaller (six beds or less - Adult Family Homes) or much larger (24+ beds - assisted living facilities). Our unique size enables us to provide excellent care for residents living with moderate to advanced dementia. We are large enough to provide the security, safety and nighttime assistance that our residents require while also being small enough to ensure that individual resident needs and preferences are never overlooked. As Goldilocks would say, the size of Windsor Gardens is "just right."

Caregiver Ratios:

Windsor Gardens boasts excellent resident-to-caregiver ratios: 6-to-1 during the daytime hours and 12-to-1 during the nighttime hours. We provide awake care staff 24/7. These resident-to-caregiver ratios are among the best available.


Low Caregiver Turnover:

Windsor Gardens leads the industry in its ability to find and maintain an outstanding care staff. Several of our caregivers have worked with us for 5+ years. This characteristic ensures that our residents receive care from an experienced staff that is intimately familiar with each of their personal needs. Windsor Gardens leads the industry in its ability to find and maintain an outstanding care staff. In an industry rife with caregiver burnout and high caregiver turnover, Windsor Gardens is a rare exception.

Secure Environment: 

For residents requiring memory care, safety is a top priority. At Windsor Gardens, exterior gates and a secured facility ensure that our residents are able to move freely within the community, but prevented from leaving without supervision. Additionally, our secured outdoor deck and walking paths allow residents an opportunity to get fresh air and burn some energy.


Consistency:

Windsor Gardens has been locally owned-and-operated by the same family for 30 years. Schedule a tour today to learn more about what makes Windsor Gardens an ideal and unique choice for residents requiring memory care.

Environment:

This one-acre gated community offers residents in their old age a place where they can retain their dignity, comfort, and respect. Our exclusive community boasts walking paths amidst old trees and varied shrubbery, along with outdoor patios. These commodities allow our residents to enjoy the outdoors in spring and summer in an unrestricted, yet safe and secure environment.
